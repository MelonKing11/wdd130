Final Project

For the WDD 130 final project, our class worked in small teams to plan, design, and build complete websites. Each of us rotated through different roles: I served as the lead developer on one site, a junior developer on two others, and the client for a site built to my specifications. Below are the four websites I helped bring to life.

The Websites

Lead Developer

FOOTBALL — Learn the Beautiful Game

An educational hub that explains the fundamentals of football (soccer): rules, positions, terminology, equipment, and basic strategy for beginners and fans alike. As lead developer, I owned the site architecture, layout, and final build.
